The team

The project consisted of creating an application for the iOS platform within the Apple Developer Academy | Senac with a team of 1 Product Designer - my role - and 2 iOS developers.

My work consisted of, together with the developers:

  • Find an area of interest with opportunities for an application.
  • Determine the target audience and carry out qualitative or quantitative research.
  • Create the commercial and monetization structure of the app.
  • Structure the information architecture, navigation flow and wireframes.
  • Branding creation - including naming, logo, colour palette, typography and iconography.
  • Development of high fidelity and handoff screens for developers.
  • Post-launch monitoring - metrics, usability tests and exposure.

The Research

After several meetings around the theme, we arrived at the cosplay market, which consists of creating clothing focused on characters from series, games, movies, etc.

We saw here the possibility of an audience that spends a large part of their time and money on these costumes but does not have any type of application that meets their creative or dissemination process.

We then started with an immersion process that was summarized in 3 steps:

  • Face-to-face interviews with cosplayers to find possible needs within this market.
  • Quantitative forms distributed in national and international communities. This survey was created based on the insights gained during the first interviews and led to a higher mapping of the market.
  • Interviews through Discord, Skype and WhatsApp with cosplayers acquired through the forms. This interview had a more specific character, focusing on problems already encountered in the previous steps. Both casual cosplayers and influencers with more than 10,000 followers on social media were interviewed.
